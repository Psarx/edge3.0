Learn More About The Edge+

Edge+ stands as the flagship event hosted by the ACM student chapter at FISAT, for the past 3 years offering valuable opportunities for learning, development, and personal growth. The event provides an exceptional platform for enhancing technical knowledge and serves as an opportunity to inspire and mold young minds.

Meet Our Sponsor

Jubeerich Consultancy is a leading overseas education consultancy established in the year 1999 with its Corporate Office at Kaloor, Kochi. They have been successfully assisting students in pursuing overseas education to almost all major study destinations. Their Head Office is in New Zealand, Corporate office in Kaloor,Cochin with 12 branches across Kerala and branches in Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. Other major International offices are located in the UK, Canada, Australia, and Dubai.
Being in this industry for the past 25 years,they do have a goodwill and brand value, which will always be upheld. They do have a 99% visa approval rate representing 40+ Countries , 4000+ Universities with 100% free services,Free registration and Free IELTS coaching (ONLINE/OFFLINE).

Why EDGE+ 3.0

Participating in Edge+ is an invaluable opportunity for learning, personal growth, and networking. It provides exposure to emerging technologies, inspires with industry leaders, and offers the chance to kickstart your career. Additionally, it's a fun and engaging experience, making it a must for anyone looking to advance in the tech world.

Workshops

Edge+ workshops offer hands-on learning and practical skills in various tech-related subjects, helping participants gain in-depth knowledge and expertise.

Coding competition

Edge+ hosts a competitive coding event where participants can showcase their programming prowess, solving challenging problems and vying for recognition and prizes.

Pro show

Edge+ boasts an exhilarating pro show, featuring the FMC band, dynamic dance performances, and a high-energy DJ show, ensuring a night of entertainment and fun.

EDGE+ Workshops

Web Development Workshop

Dive into the fundamentals of web development. Develop the skills to create responsive and engaging websites, covering everything from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and beyond.

AR Workshop

Explore the exciting world of Augmented Reality (AR). Learn to create interactive digital experiences that blend the virtual and real worlds, opening doors to innovative applications in various fields.

Cyber Security Workshop

Enhance your digital security skills in this workshop. Gain insights into protecting data, identifying threats, and ensuring the integrity of online systems in an increasingly connected world.

UI/UX Workshop

Discover the art of User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design. Learn how to craft user-friendly interfaces that provide an intuitive and enjoyable digital experience for users.

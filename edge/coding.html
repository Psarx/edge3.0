ABOUT COMPETITION

Join the Edge+ 3.0 Coding Competition and prove your coding prowess. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned coder, this is your chance to compete for a substantial prize pool of ₹10,000.

Prizes:
 
- 1st Place: ₹5,000  
- 2nd Place: ₹3,000

  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow enthusiasts.
  • Certification:Get a certificate upon participation.
  • Activity points:Earn activity points.
